Dr. Zeenat Safdar is a distinguished pulmonologist specializing in pulmonary arterial hypertension (PAH) and idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis (IPF). She currently serves as a Professor of Medicine at the Houston Methodist Academic Institute and is the Director of Clinical Research and the Pulmonary Hypertension Program at the Houston Methodist Hospital Lung Center in Houston, Texas. ([scholars.houstonmethodist.org](https://scholars.houstonmethodist.org/en/persons/zeenat-safdar?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Safdar has an extensive background in pulmonary medicine, with a particular focus on PAH and IPF. She has held significant roles in both clinical and research capacities, contributing to advancements in the understanding and treatment of pulmonary diseases. Her leadership positions include serving as Chair of the Young Council at the Pulmonary Vascular Research Institute and as Pulmonology Chair at the Pulmonary Hypertension Association Online University. ([scholars.houstonmethodist.org](https://scholars.houstonmethodist.org/en/persons/zeenat-safdar?utm_source=openai))
