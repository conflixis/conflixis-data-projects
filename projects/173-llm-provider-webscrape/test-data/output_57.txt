Dr. Mohamed O. Othman is a distinguished gastroenterologist specializing in advanced endoscopic interventions, biliary tract diseases, and pancreatic disorders. He currently serves as the Chief of the Gastroenterology and Hepatology Section at Baylor College of Medicine and holds the William T. Butler Endowed Chair for Distinguished Faculty. ([bcm.edu](https://www.bcm.edu/people-search/mohamed-othman-27990?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

With over two decades of experience, Dr. Othman has made significant contributions to the field of gastroenterology through clinical practice, research, and education. His expertise encompasses advanced endoscopic procedures, including endoscopic submucosal dissection (ESD) and peroral endoscopic myotomy (POEM), aimed at providing minimally invasive treatment options for complex gastrointestinal conditions. ([stlukeshealth.org](https://www.stlukeshealth.org/resources/excellence-in-gastroenterology-and-gi-surgery?utm_source=openai))
