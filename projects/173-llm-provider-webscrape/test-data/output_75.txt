Dr. John A. Osborne is a board-certified cardiologist specializing in cardiovascular disease. He is the Director and Founder of State of the Heart Cardiology, based in Southlake, Texas. ([doximity.com](https://www.doximity.com/pub/john-osborne-md?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

With over 30 years of experience in cardiology, Dr. Osborne has dedicated his career to the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of cardiovascular diseases. His clinical interests encompass preventive cardiology, metabolic syndrome, hypertension, congestive heart failure, cholesterol disorders, and cardiovascular genetics. ([personallyyoursmd.com](https://personallyyoursmd.com/cardiology-heart-health?utm_source=openai))

**Education and Training**

- **Undergraduate Education:** Bachelor of Science with honors from Pennsylvania State University, 1983.
- **Medical Education:** Doctor of Medicine (MD), magna cum laude, from Sidney Kimmel Medical College at Thomas Jefferson University, 1990.
- **Doctoral Education:** Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Cardiovascular Physiology from Thomas Jefferson University, 1990.
- **Postdoctoral Training:**
  - Internship and Residency in Internal Medicine at Brigham and Women's Hospital, Harvard Medical School, 1990-1993.
  - Fellowship in Cardiovascular Disease at Brigham and Women's Hospital, Harvard Medical School, 1993-1997. ([personallyyoursmd.com](https://personallyyoursmd.com/cardiology-heart-health?utm_source=openai))
