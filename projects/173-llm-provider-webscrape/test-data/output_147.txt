Dr. Michael S. Hisey is a board-certified orthopedic spine surgeon specializing in minimally invasive and motion-preserving techniques. He currently serves as an orthopedic surgeon at the Texas Back Institute. ([texasback.com](https://texasback.com/physician/michael-s-hisey/?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Hisey has over two decades of experience in orthopedic spine surgery, focusing on advanced, minimally invasive procedures to treat spinal disorders. He emphasizes returning patients to productive, pain-free activity, holding surgery as a last option. ([texasback.com](https://texasback.com/physician/michael-s-hisey/?utm_source=openai))
