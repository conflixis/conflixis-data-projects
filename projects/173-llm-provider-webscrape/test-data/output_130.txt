**Aaron S. Farberg, MD – Professional Profile (Dermatology and Mohs Micrographic Surgery, Chief Medical Officer at Bare Dermatology)**

**Overview**

Dr. Aaron S. Farberg is a double board-certified dermatologist and fellowship-trained Mohs surgeon specializing in skin cancer, inflammatory diseases, and cosmetic dermatology. He serves as the Chief Medical Officer at Bare Dermatology in Dallas, Texas, and holds adjunct clinical assistant professor positions at Baylor Scott & White Health System, Texas A&M College of Medicine, and the University of North Texas Health Science Center. ([practiceupdate.com](https://www.practiceupdate.com/author/aaron-farberg/1717?utm_source=openai))

**Education and Training**

- **Undergraduate:** Bachelor of Science in Neuroscience and Behavioral Biology, summa cum laude, Emory University, 2006. ([southbeachsymposium.org](https://www.southbeachsymposium.org/faculty/aaron-s-farberg-md/?utm_source=openai))
- **Medical School:** Doctor of Medicine, University of Michigan Medical School, 2012. ([southbeachsymposium.org](https://www.southbeachsymposium.org/faculty/aaron-s-farberg-md/?utm_source=openai))
- **Residency:** Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery, University of Michigan Medical School, 2012–2015. ([southbeachsymposium.org](https://www.southbeachsymposium.org/faculty/aaron-s-farberg-md/?utm_source=openai))
- **Fellowship:** Clinical Research Fellow, National Society for Cutaneous Medicine, 2015–2016. ([southbeachsymposium.org](https://www.southbeachsymposium.org/faculty/aaron-s-farberg-md/?utm_source=openai))
