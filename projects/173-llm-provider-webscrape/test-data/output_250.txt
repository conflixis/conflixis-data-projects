**Aaron Farberg, MD – Professional Profile (Dermatology and Mohs Micrographic Surgery, Chief Medical Officer at Bare Dermatology)**

**Overview**

Dr. Aaron S. Farberg is a double board-certified dermatologist and fellowship-trained Mohs surgeon specializing in skin cancer, inflammatory diseases, and cosmetic dermatology. He serves as the Chief Medical Officer at Bare Dermatology in Dallas, Texas, and is affiliated with Baylor University Medical Center. Dr. Farberg has been recognized as one of Dallas’s leading dermatologists, recently ranked by his peers in *Newsweek* as the #1 medical dermatologist in Texas and #6 in the United States. ([barederm.com](https://www.barederm.com/our-clinicians/aaron-farberg/?utm_source=openai))

**Education and Training**

- **Undergraduate Education:** Summa cum laude graduate of Emory University.
- **Medical School:** University of Michigan Medical School (2012).
- **Residency:**
  - Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery at the University of Michigan Medical School (2012–2015).
  - Dermatology at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai Hospital, served as chief resident (2016–2019).
- **Fellowships:**
  - Clinical Research Fellowship in association with the National Society for Cutaneous Medicine and New York University (2015–2016).
  - Mohs Micrographic Surgery at Arkansas Dermatology (2019–2020). ([bswhealth.com](https://www.bswhealth.com/physician/aaron-farberg?utm_source=openai))
