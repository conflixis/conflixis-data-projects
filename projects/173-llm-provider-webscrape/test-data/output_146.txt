**Aaron K. Calodney, MD – Professional Profile (Pain Medicine Specialist and Current Title/Role)**

Dr. Aaron K. Calodney is a board-certified anesthesiologist and pain medicine specialist currently serving as the Director of Clinical Research at Precision Spine Care in Tyler, Texas. ([napapainconference.com](https://napapainconference.com/member/aaron-k-calodney/?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Calodney has an extensive background in anesthesiology and pain management, with a particular focus on interventional spine procedures and chronic pain management. He has held leadership positions in several professional organizations, including serving as Past President of both the American Society of Interventional Pain Physicians (ASIPP) and the Texas Pain Society. Additionally, he has been actively involved in clinical research and education, contributing to numerous publications and delivering over 250 invited lectures nationally and internationally. ([napapainconference.com](https://napapainconference.com/member/aaron-k-calodney/?utm_source=openai))
