Dr. William F. Peacock IV, MD, is a distinguished emergency medicine physician currently serving as Professor, Research Director, and Vice Chair for Research in the Department of Emergency Medicine at Baylor College of Medicine in Houston, Texas. ([bcm.edu](https://www.bcm.edu/people-search/william-peacock-28474?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

With over 39 years of experience in emergency medicine, Dr. Peacock has made significant contributions to cardiovascular research and emergency care. He has authored more than 800 peer-reviewed publications and co-edited several textbooks on cardiac emergencies and heart failure management. ([pri-med.com](https://www.pri-med.com/globals/faculty/p/peacockwfrank?utm_source=openai))
