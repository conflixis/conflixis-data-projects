**Reynolds M. Delgado III, MD – Professional Profile (Cardiovascular Disease Specialist and Medical Director of Mechanical Assist Devices in Heart Failure)**

**Overview**

Dr. Reynolds M. Delgado III is a distinguished cardiologist specializing in heart failure and heart transplantation. He serves as the Medical Director of Mechanical Assist Devices in Heart Failure at The Texas Heart Institute and practices at Baylor St. Luke's Medical Center in Houston, Texas. With over three decades of experience, Dr. Delgado has made significant contributions to the fields of heart failure management, mechanical circulatory support, and cardiac transplantation. ([texasheart.org](https://www.texasheart.org/people/reynolds-delgado-iii/?utm_source=openai))
