**Dr. Mazin Issa Foteh – Professional Profile (Vascular Surgery, Director of Aortic Therapy and Innovation)**

**Overview**
Dr. Mazin Issa Foteh is a board-certified vascular surgeon with extensive experience in the diagnosis and management of vascular diseases. A native of Houston, Texas, he has been instrumental in pioneering advanced vascular procedures and has a strong background in clinical research. As of August 2022, Dr. Foteh serves as the Director of Aortic Therapy and Innovation at Baylor Scott & White/The Heart Hospital of Plano and is the Co-director of the Aortic Clinic. ([cd-prod.bswhealth.com](https://cd-prod.bswhealth.com/physician/mazin-foteh?utm_source=openai))
