Dr. Richard D. Guyer is a board-certified orthopedic spine surgeon specializing in spinal surgery. He is the Co-founder of the Texas Back Institute and serves as the Director of their Spine Fellowship Program. Additionally, Dr. Guyer is the Co-director of the Center for Disc Replacement at the Texas Back Institute. ([texasback.com](https://texasback.com/physician/richard-d-guyer/?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Guyer has been instrumental in advancing artificial disc replacement surgery in the United States, leading clinical trials and performing pioneering procedures. He has held leadership roles in various professional organizations and has contributed extensively to spine health research and education. ([centerfordiscreplacement.com](https://centerfordiscreplacement.com/about-us/richard-d-guyer/?utm_source=openai))
