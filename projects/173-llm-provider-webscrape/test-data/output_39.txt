**Dr. Keith A. Heier – Professional Profile (Orthopaedic Surgery, Foot & Ankle Specialist)**

**Overview**

Dr. Keith A. Heier is a board-certified orthopedic surgeon specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of foot and ankle conditions. With over 30 years of experience, he has extensive expertise in both non-operative and operative management of sports injuries, fractures, arthritis, congenital deformities, trauma, and joint replacements. Dr. Heier is recognized for his proficiency in complex reconstructions, including fusions, total ankle replacements, and arthroscopy. He is among the select orthopedic surgeons nationwide who are fellowship-trained in foot and ankle surgery and hold subspecialty certification in sports medicine. ([orthotexas.com](https://www.orthotexas.com/dr-heier/?utm_source=openai))
