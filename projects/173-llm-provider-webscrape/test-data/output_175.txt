Dr. Jeffrey Whitman is a distinguished ophthalmologist specializing in cataract and refractive surgery. He serves as the President and Chief Surgeon at Key-Whitman Eye Center in Dallas, Texas. ([keywhitman.com](https://keywhitman.com/doctors/jeffrey-whitman-m-d-/?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

With over 40 years of experience, Dr. Whitman has performed more than 100,000 lens implant and laser refractive surgeries. He was involved in the original FDA trials for Toric LASIK treatment and continues to participate in clinical trials for corneal inlays and premium lenses combined with femtosecond laser treatments. ([bswhealth.com](https://www.bswhealth.com/physician/jeffrey-whitman?utm_source=openai))
