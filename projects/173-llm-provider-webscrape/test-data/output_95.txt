Dr. Seemal R. Desai is a board-certified dermatologist specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of skin, hair, and nail conditions. He currently serves as the President of the American Academy of Dermatology (AAD) for the 2024-2025 term. ([aad.org](https://www.aad.org/news/seemal-desai-new-president-aad?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Desai is a distinguished dermatologist with extensive experience in clinical practice, research, and leadership within the dermatology community. He is the Founder and Medical Director of Innovative Dermatology, a private practice in Plano, Texas, and holds the position of Clinical Assistant Professor of Dermatology at the University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center. ([innovative-dermatology.com](https://innovative-dermatology.com/about-us/providers/seemal-r-desai/?utm_source=openai), [utswmed.org](https://utswmed.org/doctors/seemal-desai/?utm_source=openai))
