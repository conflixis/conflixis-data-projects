**Angela Yen Moore, MD – Professional Profile (Dermatology and Dermatopathology)**

**Overview**

Dr. Angela Yen Moore is a board-certified dermatologist and dermatopathologist with over 30 years of experience. She specializes in diagnosing and treating a wide range of skin conditions, including acne, rosacea, psoriasis, and skin cancers. Dr. Moore is dedicated to providing efficient and high-quality medical, surgical, and cosmetic dermatology services to patients of all ages.
