**Joseph Jankovic, M.D. – Professional Profile (Neurology, Professor of Neurology, Distinguished Chair in Movement Disorders, Director of the Parkinson's Disease Center and Movement Disorders Clinic)**

**Overview**

Dr. Joseph Jankovic is a renowned neurologist with extensive expertise in movement disorders. He serves as a Professor of Neurology and holds the Distinguished Chair in Movement Disorders at Baylor College of Medicine. As the founder and director of the Parkinson's Disease Center and Movement Disorders Clinic (PDCMDC), Dr. Jankovic has significantly advanced the understanding and treatment of various movement disorders. ([bcm.edu](https://www.bcm.edu/people-search/joseph-jankovic-23852?utm_source=openai))
