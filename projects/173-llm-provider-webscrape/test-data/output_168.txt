Dr. Zaina Al-Mohtaseb is a board-certified ophthalmologist specializing in cataract, LASIK, and cornea surgery. She currently serves as the Director of Research at Whitsett Vision Group in Houston, Texas, and holds a Clinical Associate Professor position at Baylor College of Medicine. ([whitsettvision.com](https://www.whitsettvision.com/our-doctors/zaina-al-mohtaseb-md.html?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Al-Mohtaseb has an extensive background in ophthalmology, focusing on complex vision correction surgeries. She is recognized as a key opinion leader in the field, adopting new surgical techniques and ophthalmic technologies. Her expertise encompasses a wide range of procedures, including cataract surgery, LASIK, PRK, ICL, presbyopia correction, DSEK, DMEK, collagen cross-linking, and IOL exchanges. ([whitsettvision.com](https://www.whitsettvision.com/our-doctors/zaina-al-mohtaseb-md.html?utm_source=openai))
