Dr. Nicola A. Hanania is a distinguished pulmonologist specializing in pulmonary disease, currently serving as a Professor of Medicine in the Section of Pulmonary and Critical Care Medicine at Baylor College of Medicine in Houston, Texas. He is also the Director of the Airways Clinical Research Center and Chief of the Section of Pulmonary, Critical Care, and Sleep Medicine at Ben Taub Hospital. ([bcm.edu](https://www.bcm.edu/people-search/nicola-hanania-22632?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

With over 40 years of experience in pulmonary and critical care medicine, Dr. Hanania has dedicated his career to the management and research of airway diseases, particularly asthma and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD). He oversees the Airways Clinical Research Center at Baylor College of Medicine, leading a team involved in both single-center and multi-center collaborative clinical trials. His research focuses on airway pharmacology, aiming to translate laboratory findings into clinical applications to develop new treatments. ([bcm.edu](https://www.bcm.edu/people-search/nicola-hanania-22632?utm_source=openai))
