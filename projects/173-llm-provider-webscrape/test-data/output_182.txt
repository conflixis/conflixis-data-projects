Dr. Nadege Tacha Gunn is a board-certified gastroenterologist and hepatologist specializing in liver diseases, particularly non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD) and nonalcoholic steatohepatitis (NASH). She currently serves as a Principal Investigator at Velocity Clinical Research in Waco, Texas. ([velocityclinical.com](https://velocityclinical.com/investigators/nadege-gunn-md/?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Gunn has extensive experience in gastroenterology and hepatology, with a focus on liver diseases such as NAFLD and NASH. She has authored numerous peer-reviewed journal articles and actively participates in clinical trials aimed at advancing treatments for these conditions. ([velocityclinical.com](https://velocityclinical.com/investigators/nadege-gunn-md/?utm_source=openai))
