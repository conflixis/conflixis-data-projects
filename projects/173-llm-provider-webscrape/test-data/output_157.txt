**Cedric W. Spak, MD, MPH – Professional Profile (Infectious Disease Specialist and Current Title/Role)**

Dr. Cedric W. Spak is an infectious disease specialist affiliated with Baylor Scott & White Health and a partner at Texas Centers for Infectious Disease Associates (TCIDA) in Dallas and Fort Worth, Texas. ([bswhealth.com](https://www.bswhealth.com/physician/cedric-spak?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

With nearly 20 years of experience, Dr. Spak specializes in managing complex infectious diseases, including transplant-related infections, bone and joint infections, and HIV. ([texascentersid.com](https://www.texascentersid.com/cedric-spak-md-mph-infectious-disease-forth-worth-dallas-tx.html?utm_source=openai))

**Education and Training**

- **Undergraduate:** B.A. in History and German Language, Rice University, 1994 (Cum Laude, Senior Honors Thesis)
- **Medical School:** M.D., University of Texas Southwestern Medical School, 1999
- **Internship:** Internal Medicine, University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center, 2000
- **Residency:** Internal Medicine, University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center, 2002
- **Fellowship:** Infectious Diseases, University of Washington, 2006
- **Additional Degree:** M.P.H. in Epidemiology, University of Washington, 2007 (K30 Clinical Research Training Program) ([texascentersid.com](https://www.texascentersid.com/cedric-spak-md-mph-infectious-disease-forth-worth-dallas-tx.html?utm_source=openai))
