Dr. Sachin Suresh Kukreja is a board-certified general and bariatric surgeon based in Dallas, Texas, with over 20 years of experience. He specializes in minimally invasive surgical techniques, including laparoscopic and robotic procedures, focusing on bariatric surgery and general surgery. ([dfwbariatricsurgery.com](https://www.dfwbariatricsurgery.com/about-us/dr-sachin-kukreja/?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Kukreja is the founder and director of DFW Bariatrics and General Surgery, where he offers comprehensive obesity care, including medications, diet counseling, non-surgical solutions, and a full range of surgical options. He is recognized for his expertise in minimally invasive and bariatric surgery and has been honored as a D Magazine Best Doctor for seven consecutive years as of 2024. ([dfwbariatricsurgery.com](https://www.dfwbariatricsurgery.com/about-us/dr-sachin-kukreja/?utm_source=openai))
