**Dr. Jessica Shepherd – Professional Profile (Gynecology and Current Title/Role)**

Dr. Jessica Shepherd is a board-certified obstetrician-gynecologist specializing in gynecology. She currently serves as the Chief Medical Officer at Hers, a telehealth company focused on women's health, and is the founder and CEO of Sanctum Med + Wellness, a holistic wellness practice in Dallas, Texas. ([forhers.com](https://www.forhers.com/medical-experts/jessica-shepherd-md?utm_source=openai), [jessicashepherdmd.com](https://www.jessicashepherdmd.com/about-dr-jessica-shepherd?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

With over 20 years of experience in women's health, Dr. Shepherd is dedicated to empowering women through comprehensive care that addresses physical, emotional, and lifestyle needs. She is recognized for her expertise in minimally invasive gynecologic surgery and her commitment to advancing women's health and longevity, particularly during perimenopause and menopause. ([jessicashepherdmd.com](https://www.jessicashepherdmd.com/about-dr-jessica-shepherd?utm_source=openai))
