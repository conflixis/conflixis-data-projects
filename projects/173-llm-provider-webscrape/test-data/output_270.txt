**Clinton S. Bell, MD – Professional Profile (Orthopaedic Surgery, Orthopaedic Surgeon at Arlington Orthopedic Associates, P.A.)**

**Overview**

Dr. Clinton S. Bell is a board-certified orthopaedic surgeon with over 20 years of experience specializing in general adult and pediatric orthopaedics, sports medicine, arthroscopic surgery of the knee and shoulder, hip and knee replacement surgery, orthopaedic trauma and fracture care, hand and wrist surgery, endoscopic carpal tunnel surgery, and foot and ankle surgery. He is recognized for his dedication to patient care and has been consistently selected as one of *D Magazine’s* Best Doctors in Dallas. ([bswarlington.com](https://bswarlington.com/physicians/clinton-bell/?utm_source=openai))
