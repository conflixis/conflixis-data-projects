Dr. John A. Osborne is a board-certified cardiologist specializing in cardiovascular disease, currently serving as the Director and Founder of State of the Heart Cardiology in Southlake, Texas. ([doximity.com](https://www.doximity.com/pub/john-osborne-md?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

With over 34 years of experience in cardiology, Dr. Osborne has dedicated his career to advancing cardiovascular health through clinical practice, research, and education. He is recognized for his expertise in preventive cardiology, metabolic syndrome, hypertension, congestive heart failure, cholesterol disorders, and cardiovascular genetics. ([personallyyoursmd.com](https://personallyyoursmd.com/cardiology-heart-health?utm_source=openai))

**Education and Training**

- **Undergraduate Education:** Bachelor of Science with honors from Pennsylvania State University.
- **Medical Education:** Doctor of Medicine (MD), magna cum laude, from Jefferson Medical College at Thomas Jefferson University.
- **Doctoral Education:** Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Cardiovascular Physiology from Thomas Jefferson University.
- **Postdoctoral Training:** Internship, Residency, and Research Residency in Internal Medicine, followed by a Fellowship in Cardiovascular Disease at Brigham and Women's Hospital, Harvard Medical School. ([ahahealthtech.org](https://ahahealthtech.org/panelists/panelist-1-tile05/?utm_source=openai))
