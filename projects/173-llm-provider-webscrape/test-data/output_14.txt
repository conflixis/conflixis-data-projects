**Eric S. Wieser, MD – Professional Profile (Orthopaedic Surgery of the Spine and Current Title/Role)**

Dr. Eric S. Wieser is a fellowship-trained orthopedic spine surgeon specializing in minimally invasive spinal surgery and complex cervical and lumbar spinal reconstruction. He serves as the founder and medical director of the Texas Comprehensive Spine Center at Arlington Orthopedic Associates. ([arlingtonortho.com](https://www.arlingtonortho.com/texas-comprehensive-spine-center/?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

A lifelong Texan, Dr. Wieser has been practicing in North Texas since 2004. He co-founded the Baylor Orthopedic and Spine Hospital in Arlington and has been consistently recognized by his peers as a "Top Doc" in various Texas publications. ([arlingtonortho.com](https://www.arlingtonortho.com/physician/dr-eric-wieser/?utm_source=openai))

**Education and Training**

- **High School:** Valedictorian, China Spring High School, China Spring, TX, 1990
- **Undergraduate:** Bachelor of Science in Biology with honors, Trinity University, San Antonio, TX, 1994
- **Medical School:** Doctor of Medicine with honors, Baylor College of Medicine, Houston, TX, 1998
- **Internship:** General Surgery, Vanderbilt University Medical Center, Nashville, TN, 1998-1999
- **Residency:** Orthopaedic Surgery, Vanderbilt University Medical Center, Nashville, TN, 1999-2003
- **Fellowship:** Spine Surgery, UCLA School of Medicine, Los Angeles, CA, 2003-2004
