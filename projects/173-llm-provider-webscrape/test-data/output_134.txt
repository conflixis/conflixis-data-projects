Dr. Oluwatosin Urowoli Smith, MD, is a board-certified ophthalmologist specializing in glaucoma. She currently serves as a partner physician at Glaucoma Associates of Texas in Dallas and holds the position of Clinical Associate Professor of Ophthalmology at UT Southwestern Medical Center. ([youngmdconnect.com](https://youngmdconnect.com/articles/2022-may-june/outstanding-female-leader-in-ophthalmology-oluwatosin-smith-md?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Smith has over 29 years of experience in ophthalmology, with a focus on the medical, laser, and surgical treatment of glaucoma and cataracts. She is actively involved in clinical research, industry collaborations, and global outreach initiatives. ([youngmdconnect.com](https://youngmdconnect.com/articles/2022-may-june/outstanding-female-leader-in-ophthalmology-oluwatosin-smith-md?utm_source=openai))
