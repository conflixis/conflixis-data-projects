Dr. Athar M. Qureshi is a distinguished pediatric cardiologist specializing in interventional cardiology. He currently serves as the Medical Director of the CE Mullins Cardiac Catheterization Laboratories and Program Director of the Interventional Cardiology Fellowship at Texas Children's Hospital. Additionally, he holds the position of Professor of Pediatrics at Baylor College of Medicine. ([texaschildrens.org](https://www.texaschildrens.org/find-a-provider/athar-m-qureshi-md?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Qureshi has an extensive background in pediatric cardiology, with over two decades of experience. His expertise encompasses a wide range of interventional procedures for congenital heart diseases in both children and adults. He is recognized for his contributions to advancing catheterization techniques and has been a pivotal figure in training the next generation of interventional cardiologists.
