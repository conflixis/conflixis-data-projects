Dr. Allen F. Morey is a board-certified urologist specializing in reconstructive urology, currently practicing at Urology Clinics of North Texas in McKinney, Texas. ([drallenmorey.com](https://drallenmorey.com/?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Morey has over three decades of experience in urologic trauma and reconstructive surgery. He served 20 years in the U.S. Army Medical Corps, including roles as Chief of Urology at Brooke Army Medical Center and Urology Consultant to the Army Surgeon General. Following his military service, he was a Professor and Distinguished Chair in Reconstructive Urology at the University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center from 2007 to 2023. ([drallenmorey.com](https://drallenmorey.com/about/?utm_source=openai))
