Dr. Craig F. Teller is a board-certified dermatologist practicing in Bellaire, Texas. He is the founder and a practicing physician at Bellaire Dermatology Associates. ([boardcertified.com](https://www.boardcertified.com/texas/bellaire/physician/craig-teller?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Teller has been practicing dermatology since 1995. He is recognized for his expertise in advanced cosmetic procedures, psoriasis care, phlebology, laser treatments, and managing challenging skin conditions. He has delivered over 100 national presentations and lectures on these topics. ([boardcertified.com](https://www.boardcertified.com/texas/bellaire/physician/craig-teller?utm_source=openai))
