Dr. Aldona J. Spiegel is a board-certified plastic and reconstructive surgeon specializing in advanced breast reconstruction procedures. She currently serves as the Director of the Center for Breast Restoration at Houston Methodist Hospital's Institute for Reconstructive Surgery. Additionally, she holds the position of Professor of Clinical Surgery at Weill Cornell Medical College and is the John F. Jr. and Carolyn Bookout Professor in Surgical Innovation and Technology. Dr. Spiegel also serves as the Chief of the Division of Surgical Innovation at Houston Methodist Hospital. ([scholars.houstonmethodist.org](https://scholars.houstonmethodist.org/en/persons/aldona-spiegel?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Spiegel has over 20 years of experience in plastic and reconstructive surgery, with a primary focus on advanced breast reconstruction techniques. She specializes in autologous muscle-preserving perforator flaps and has performed over 4,000 microsurgical reconstructive procedures. Her expertise includes composite breast reconstruction after nipple-sparing mastectomy, direct-to-implant breast reconstruction, and fat grafting procedures. Dr. Spiegel has also pioneered techniques for restoring sensation in microsurgical breast procedures and has contributed significantly to the field of breast neurotization. ([scholars.houstonmethodist.org](https://scholars.houstonmethodist.org/en/persons/aldona-spiegel?utm_source=openai))
