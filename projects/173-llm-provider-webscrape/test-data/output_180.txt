**Dr. Natalie Ann Wright – Professional Profile (Dermatology, Board-Certified Dermatologist)**

**Overview**

Dr. Natalie Ann Wright is a board-certified dermatologist based in Plano, Texas, specializing in the diagnosis and management of autoimmune skin conditions, as well as general dermatologic issues such as rashes, acne, and skin cancer. She is the founder and lead dermatologist at Natalie Wright Dermatology. ([wrightderm.com](https://www.wrightderm.com/?utm_source=openai))

**Education and Training**

- **Undergraduate Education:** Graduated with honors from Texas Christian University, Fort Worth, Texas. ([wrightderm.com](https://www.wrightderm.com/about-us?utm_source=openai))
- **Medical School:** University of Texas Medical School at Houston, 2010. ([bswhealth.com](https://www.bswhealth.com/physician/natalie-wright?utm_source=openai))
- **Internship:** Internal Medicine, University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center, Dallas, 2011. ([bswhealth.com](https://www.bswhealth.com/physician/natalie-wright?utm_source=openai))
- **Residency:** Dermatology, State University of New York Downstate Medical Center, Brooklyn, 2014. ([bswhealth.com](https://www.bswhealth.com/physician/natalie-wright?utm_source=openai))
- **Fellowship:** Dermatology-Rheumatology/Autoimmune Disease of the Skin, Brigham and Women's Hospital and Harvard Medical School, Boston, 2015. ([doximity.com](https://www.doximity.com/pub/natalie-wright-md?utm_source=openai))
