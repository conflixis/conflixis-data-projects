**Stanley Cohen, MD – Professional Profile (Rheumatology and Current Title/Role)**

Dr. Stanley B. Cohen is a board-certified rheumatologist and internist affiliated with Baylor Scott & White Health. He serves as a Clinical Professor in the Department of Internal Medicine at the University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center and Co-Director of the Division of Rheumatology at Presbyterian Hospital, Dallas. Additionally, Dr. Cohen is Co-Medical Director of the Metroplex Clinical Research Center. ([rheumatologyacademy.org](https://rheumatologyacademy.org/profiles/stanley-b-cohen-md/L6G9wA/biography/?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

With over four decades of experience, Dr. Cohen has been a pivotal figure in rheumatology since joining Rheumatology Associates in 1975. His extensive career encompasses clinical practice, academic contributions, and leadership roles within professional organizations. Notably, he is a past president of the American College of Rheumatology and was honored in 2016 with the designation of Master by the same institution. ([rheumatologyacademy.org](https://rheumatologyacademy.org/profiles/stanley-b-cohen-md/L6G9wA/biography/?utm_source=openai))
