**Stephen E. Hohmann, MD – Professional Profile (Vascular Surgery Specialist and Current Title/Role)**

Dr. Stephen E. Hohmann is a board-certified vascular surgeon affiliated with Baylor Scott & White Health in Dallas, Texas. He serves as the Program Director of the Vascular Surgery Fellowship at Baylor University Medical Center and holds the position of Clinical Assistant Professor at Texas A&M Health Science Center. ([texasvascularassociates.com](https://texasvascularassociates.com/physicians-staff/stephen-e-hohmann-md-facs/?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Hohmann has over 20 years of experience in vascular surgery, specializing in the diagnosis and management of arterial, venous, and lymphatic system disorders. He is recognized for his expertise in treating conditions such as arteriovenous malformations, peripheral vascular disease, and aortic aneurysms. ([bswhealth.com](https://www.bswhealth.com/physician/stephen-hohmann?utm_source=openai))
