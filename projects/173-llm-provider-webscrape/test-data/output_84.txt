**Marc Richard Dean, MD – Professional Profile (Otolaryngology, Board-Certified Otolaryngologist)**

**Overview**

Dr. Marc Richard Dean is a board-certified otolaryngologist specializing in ear and sinus diseases, with a particular focus on Eustachian tube disorders. He practices full-time at the Ear & Sinus Institute in Fort Worth, Texas, and holds clinical professorships at both Louisiana State University Health Sciences Center Shreveport and Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center. Dr. Dean is recognized for pioneering treatments such as Eustachian Tube Balloon Dilation and has contributed significantly to the field of otorhinology and telemedicine. ([earandsinusinstitute.com](https://earandsinusinstitute.com/?utm_source=openai), [thcotolaryngology.com](https://www.thcotolaryngology.com/doctors/dr-marc-dean/?utm_source=openai))
