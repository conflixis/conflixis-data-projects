Dr. Peter A. McCullough is an internist and cardiologist based in Dallas, Texas, with over three decades of experience in the medical field. He currently serves as the Chief Scientific Officer for The Wellness Company, focusing on innovative healthcare solutions and telemedicine services. ([twc.health](https://www.twc.health/pages/dr-peter-mccullough/?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. McCullough has an extensive background in internal medicine and cardiology, with a particular emphasis on preventive cardiology and lipidology. He has held significant academic and clinical positions, including Vice Chief of Internal Medicine at Baylor University Medical Center and Chief Academic and Scientific Officer at St. John Providence Health System. Throughout his career, Dr. McCullough has been actively involved in research, publishing over 1,000 articles and contributing to top-tier medical journals. ([isom.ca](https://isom.ca/profile/peter-mccullough/?utm_source=openai))
