Dr. Ricardo Hugo Alvarez is a board-certified medical oncologist specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of various cancers, with a particular focus on breast cancer. He currently serves as a Medical Oncologist at Oncology Consultants in Houston, Texas. ([doctor.webmd.com](https://doctor.webmd.com/doctor/ricardo-alvarez-b24f5871-2de7-460b-9d84-b138799a2621-overview?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Alvarez has over 34 years of experience in medical oncology, with a significant emphasis on breast cancer treatment and research. He has held leadership roles, including Medical Director of the Breast Center and Medical Director of Cancer Research at Cancer Treatment Centers of America (CTCA). His work has been widely published in peer-reviewed journals, and he has been actively involved in clinical trials aimed at advancing breast cancer therapies. ([biography.omicsonline.org](https://biography.omicsonline.org/united-states-of-america/cancer-treatment-centers-of-america/ricardo-alvarez-572448?utm_source=openai))
