**Luis A. Mignucci, M.D. – Professional Profile (Neurological Surgery and Current Title/Role)**

Dr. Luis A. Mignucci is a board-certified neurosurgeon specializing in neurological surgery, with a focus on spine surgery. He is the founder and lead surgeon at Neurospine Surgical Consultants in Plano, Texas. ([neurospinesurgical.com](https://neurospinesurgical.com/dr-luis/?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

With over 30 years of experience, Dr. Mignucci was the first board-certified neurosurgeon in the Dallas-Fort Worth area to complete a formal fellowship in spine surgery at the Cleveland Clinic Foundation. He has been providing medical care at Neurospine Surgical Consultants for more than three decades, offering advanced treatments for various spinal conditions. ([dmagazine.com](https://www.dmagazine.com/publications/d-magazine/2022/february/face-of-neurosurgery-spine/?utm_source=openai))
