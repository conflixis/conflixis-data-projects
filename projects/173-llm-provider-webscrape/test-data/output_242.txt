Dr. Jerome P. Lisk is a board-certified neurologist specializing in movement disorders, currently serving as the Director of Movement Disorders at MD Neurology in Denton and Flower Mound, Texas. ([mdneurology.net](https://www.mdneurology.net/jerome_lisk.html?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

With over 26 years of experience in neurology, Dr. Lisk has dedicated his career to the diagnosis and treatment of movement disorders, including Parkinson's disease and dystonia. He has been a principal investigator in several national clinical research trials and has held various academic and advisory positions throughout his career. ([mdneurology.net](https://www.mdneurology.net/jerome_lisk.html?utm_source=openai))
