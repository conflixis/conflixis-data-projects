**Dr. Oluwatosin Urowoli Smith – Professional Profile (Ophthalmology, Partner Physician at Glaucoma Associates of Texas)**

**Overview**

Dr. Oluwatosin Urowoli Smith is a distinguished ophthalmologist specializing in glaucoma and retinal diseases. With over 29 years of experience, she serves as a partner physician at Glaucoma Associates of Texas in Dallas. Dr. Smith is also a Clinical Associate Professor of Ophthalmology at UT Southwestern Medical College. She is actively involved in clinical research, serves on multiple advisory boards, and is a member of several professional societies. Additionally, she oversees the Cure Glaucoma Foundation, reflecting her commitment to global outreach and education.
