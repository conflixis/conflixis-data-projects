Dr. Jack E. Zigler is a board-certified orthopedic spine surgeon specializing in spine surgery at the Texas Back Institute in Plano, Texas. He serves as Co-Director of the Center for Disc Replacement and Co-Director of the Spine Fellowship Program at the institute. ([texasback.com](https://texasback.com/physician/jack-e-zigler/?utm_source=openai))

**Overview**

Dr. Zigler has over 30 years of experience in spine surgery, with a particular focus on motion-preserving spinal technologies, including lumbar and cervical disc replacements. He performed the first ProDisc-L lumbar artificial disc replacement in the United States in October 2001 and has been instrumental in advancing disc arthroplasty techniques globally. ([texasback.com](https://texasback.com/physician/jack-e-zigler/?utm_source=openai))
