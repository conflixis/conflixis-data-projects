**Dr. Carl Lauryssen – Professional Profile (Neurological Surgery, Neurosurgeon at Baylor Scott & White Medical Center)**

**Overview**

Dr. Carl Lauryssen is a board-certified neurosurgeon with over 36 years of experience specializing in neurological surgery. He is affiliated with Baylor Scott & White Medical Center and has a distinguished career focusing on minimally invasive spine surgery, motion preservation, and the treatment of complex spinal disorders. ([health.usnews.com](https://health.usnews.com/doctors/carl-lauryssen-877844?utm_source=openai))

**Education and Training**

- **Medical Degree:** University of Cape Town Faculty of Medicine, South Africa, 1988
- **Residency:** Neurosurgery, University of Calgary, Alberta, Canada, 1991–1995
